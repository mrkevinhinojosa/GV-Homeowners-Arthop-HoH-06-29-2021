After more than a year, Art Hop is BACK in downtown Fresno! Art Hop is a community experience hosted by the Fresno Arts Council and featuring street vendors, food trucks, and more than 15 different art galleries and museums open to the public. The event takes place the first Thursday of each month and the first Art Hop since the pandemic began will be held this Thursday, July 1.  There will be no charge for parking at the M Street Arts Complex after 6:00 p.m.

 

Location: 1419 M Street,  Fresno, CA 93712.
